Membership

We're always looking for new members to support Project North Star. Membership enables you to participate in all aspects of the project, from attendance at our quarterly meetings, to fundraising, joining the Board of Directors, or simply keeping updated through our newsletter, the NStar Chronicle.

We use the funds raised through membership fees to purchase tools and supplies for the restoration effort, and to support the work of our association.

Membership application/renewal

Membership costs $25.00 Cdn per year and the membership year is 01 April to 31 March. Join anytime by completing the form at the link below.
