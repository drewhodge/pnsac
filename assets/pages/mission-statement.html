Our Mission

The Mission of the Project North Star Association of Canada (PNSAC) is to assist the Canada Aviation Museum in the restoration of specific aircraft in its collection, as well as other related tasks that fall within its capabilities.

The Association will supply and organize resources essential to the restoration process. These include members skilled in areas such as aviation trades, research, administration and work management, engineering, information technology and fundraising.

The PNSAC is dedicated to the preservation of Canada's aviation heritage.
